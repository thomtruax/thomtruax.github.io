Thom Truax

About

Thom Truax is a writer-performer. He is currently developing the horror-comedy feature Slay of the Dead Vol. 1 for producer Michael Showalter, with Trixie Mattel and Katya set to star. On the television side, his pilot Child of God was a semi-finalist in the ScreenCraft Screenwriting Fellowship 2023. A graduate of USC’s Writing for Film & Television program, his short film Sorry, I Can’t Talk Right Now premiered at Outfest 2022.
