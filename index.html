THOM TRUAX

About

Thom Truax is a writer‑performer. He is currently developing the horror‑comedy feature Slay of the Dead Vol. 1 for producer Michael Showalter. On the television side, his pilot Child of God was a semi‑finalist in the ScreenCraft Screenwriting Fellowship 2023. A graduate of USC’s Writing for Film & Television program, his short film Sorry, I Can’t Talk Right Now premiered at Outfest 2022.
